About Me

Hi, glad you made it to my page. My name is Zach Garcia (my friends just call me "Z", haha). I'm a 26 year-old born and raised in Chicago, currently living in Uptown in the same city. In 2015 I graduated from Loyola University with a B.S. in Environmental Science. Soon after, I moved to Malta to get my Master's degrees in Environmental Management & Sustainability and Integrated Science & Technology. It was really hot on that island! Unofortunately, after sometime working in my field I became disheartened. Many of my co-workers and employers were very dispassionate and dis-interested in the work we were doing, and I felt as though my contributions were of no consequence. Thus, I decided to change career paths and I'm now enrolled in Northwestern's coding bootcamp baby!

Enough of all that education mumbo jumbo, I'm sure you'd rather hear more about the real Z! In my spare time I like to read informational books, from books about dark matter to body language. I also dabble in the 'ol six string when I have some down time at the crib. I'm a big fan of horror movies, comedy shows, board games, ultimate firsbee, and the out doors (This sure feels like I'm writing an about me section for a dating site)! I've worked a ton of odd jobs, from knife salesman, to biodeisel lab technitian, all the way to pedi-cabber (That's a picture of me pedi-cabbing in what is hopefully the top left section of this page!)
